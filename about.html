✦ Our Story ✦

Crafted with Passion.
Served with Soul.

Since 2008, La Maison has been a sanctuary for those who believe that food is not just sustenance — it is art, culture, and memory, all on a single plate.

A legacy rooted in tradition

La Maison was founded by Chef Arnaud Dubois with a simple philosophy: let the finest ingredients speak for themselves. Growing up in Lyon, France, Arnaud spent his childhood in his grandmother's kitchen, learning that great food begins not with technique, but with love.

We source our ingredients daily from trusted local farmers and artisan producers across Tamil Nadu and beyond. Our seasonal menu changes with the harvest, ensuring every visit is a new experience.
